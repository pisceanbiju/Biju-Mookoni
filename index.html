How to make tea on a flame burner

To make tea, perform the following steps:

  1. Turn the knob toward right to light the flame burner.
  2. Place a kettle or teapot on the burner to boil a glass of water.
  3. When the water is still boiling, pour half a glass of milk into the boiling water.
  4. Add two teaspoons of tea powder and sugar (as per your requirement) into the boiling content.
  5. Stir the tea and serve hot, as shown in the figure below.

Caution: For safety, be careful when you remove the kettle or teapot from the burner while it is still hot.
